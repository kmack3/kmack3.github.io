Understanding Accommodations Reserach Project

Study Recruitment

Are you a DRS student? Or do you have a disability/chronic condition/mental health issue that affects your access to your classes? We think that you could be a great participant for our study to learn how increased transparency and communication can impact accommodation in higher education. During Winter 2022 we will ask you to:

  • Use a simple issue tracking tool to communicate about DRS accommodation requests with at least one of your professors
  • We may ask you to complete a 45-75 minute pre-interview or post-interview
  • Fill out a short (< 5 minute) survey each week.

Students will also be compensated $5 per survey that they fill out and $40/hr of interviews.

First, please fill out this screener survey if you are interested, so that we can collect a diverse sample group. If you qualify for the study and we have space for participants, a researcher will email you with more information about the study. Feel free to contact this email at kmack3@cs.washington.edu for any questions or concerns.

This project is a part of a research study being conducted at the University of Washington led by Kelly Mack under the advisement of Jennifer Mankoff. This study is IRB approved.
