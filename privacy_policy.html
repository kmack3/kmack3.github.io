Privacy Policy

Purpose

This privacy policy is to help make sure our users are comfortable with our app and understand how their data is used. This add-on was created for a research project conducted at the University of Washington. This study received IRB approval. Please reach out to the email below if you have questions. Below, "we" refers to the researchers on this project

Information collection and use

This app collects the slides from the decks that you use this add-on in. Note you need to utilize the "Correction" or "Feedback" pane in order for any logging to occur. If you don't open the plug in, your slides will not be logged. These slides that we log will be stored on your google drive and the lead researcher will be added as a collaborator to this folder. You can review this data and remove any logged data for the duration of the study. Please contact us if you have any questions/would like to remove data after the conclusion of the study.

Information shared with others

The add-on will not share any information collected with this add-on outside the research team at the University of Washington.
